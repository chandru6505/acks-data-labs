About ACKS Data Labs

ACKS Data Labs is a data services vendor supporting AI and machine learning teams with high-quality data labeling, audio transcription, and image & video annotation services.

We work on contract-based projects and help teams scale their data operations using a flexible and quality-focused workforce.

Our Services

  • Text, image, audio & video data labeling
  • Audio transcription (Indian languages & English)
  • Speech-to-text dataset preparation
  • Contract-based & pilot project support
